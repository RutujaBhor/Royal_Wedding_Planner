DECOR AND DESIGN


Design and décor is an indispensible part of the wedding ceremony. But, as he is very busy with the details of the wedding, he is not able to the scene at his leisure. While planning the wedding, most often the theme of design and décor is the first step. The hosts have to make decision such as whether it should be Indian or western. This is a vast subject as the hosts have to decide the décor for many wedding ceremonies such as bridal shower, Mehendi, sangeet, Bachelor and bachelorette parties, Roka, Haldi, Baraat and rehersal dinner.
